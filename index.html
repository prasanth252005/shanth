About Me

Hello! I'm Prasanth R, a passionate Electronics and Communication Engineering student at Karpagam Institute of Technology, Coimbatore. With a strong foundation in embedded systems, IoT, and hardware design, I'm dedicated to creating innovative solutions that bridge the gap between technology and real-world applications.

My journey in electronics spans microprocessors, embedded systems, IoT development, and PCB design. I've completed specialized training programs and have hands-on experience with various hardware platforms including Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and ESP32.
